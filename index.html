ABOUT US

We are a team of like-minded scientists whose aim is to advance human civilization and better them. Although humans have come a long way, we have stagnated. We wish to fix this and improve society. Our scientists do research on humans and living creatures to compatibilitate the genes of both species and discover new DNA. In other words, we wish to create meta-humans. Join us on this fascinating journey to progress our society forward!

OUR HISTORY

Plankton Company was founded in 1995 by Dr. Plankton Singh, our Head of Research Faculty. He had suffered an unfortunate loss which gave him the idea to create Plankton Company so that society no longer had to grieve as he did.
Since then, we have hired many talented scientists who have greatly contributed to our research and development. Plankton Company hopes to recruit more people to play a part in moving forward in the near future.
We are currently undertaking the BIO-TECH-413 Human Experiment Project (BTHEP) and have made many new discoveries thanks to our co-operative participants.

Here are some achievements that Plankton Company have attained:
1996 Best Start-Up BIOTECH Company
2001 HUMANIL Stem Cell Discovery
2013 BIOTECH LAB International Conference Contributor
